GIS MAPPING AND ANALYSIS

A GIS-based solution is used to allocate waste collection sites efficiently, promoting equal access and sustainable waste management. Smart dustbins equipped with technology detect when they are full, sending alerts to collectors for timely waste transport, reducing pollution effects, and enhancing urban development and environmental quality...
